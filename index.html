The Art of the Possible & the Why

Layered, reusable, GitHub-friendly network architecture diagrams for Cloud Solutions Architects

Design Clarity at Every Layer

This project provides a curated library of cloud network architecture diagrams built for reuse and collaboration. Each diagram is layered, version-controlled, and designed to communicate both what is being built and why — helping architects align teams, accelerate decisions, and explore what's possible.

🧩 Layered Design

Diagrams are structured by concern — network, security, identity, connectivity — so you can mix and match layers.

♻️ Reusable Patterns

Reference architectures that can be adapted across projects, customers, and cloud environments.

🐙 GitHub-Friendly

Everything lives in source control. Diagrams are diff-able, forkable, and pull-request ready.
